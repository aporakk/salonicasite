A city full of history of over 4000 years! There are many historical places to visit and museums available. All the museums are located in the center of the city and in close distance from one to another.

Gastronomy is an art in this city as it is top 100 cities in the world with the best food. Many restaurants are located throughout the city, offering a variety of dishes and cuisines. Make sure to visit them.

Thessaloniki is known for the cutlural influence it has not only in Greece, but throughout the world. Many great artists, philosophers, and people of the mind have originated from this city.

A city that never sleeps, it has one of the youngest population in Greece, andthus a great night life.
